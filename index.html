Covid-19 Approach

Important information for drivers and Customers

Health and safety tips

We’re reminding everyone who uses Rands-Ride to follow advice from public health authorities. For more information, visit the World Health Organization website.

If you’re driving

Cover your mouth and nose. Ask riders to maintain distance. Open the windows. If possible, roll down the windows to improve ventilation.

Stay at home when you feeling really sick

This is especially critical if you’re feeling sick. This will help limit the spread of the virus. Let’s help keep drivers safe and ensure that rides are available for those who need them.

Tip your driver

Drivers and delivery people are working extra hard right now, doing critical work to support your community.Please show your appreciation with a tip—a little kindness can go a long way.

Helping to keep cars clean

We are working to provide drivers with disinfectants to help them keep their cars clean. Supplies are very limited, but we’re partnering with manufacturers and distributors to source as much as possible.
